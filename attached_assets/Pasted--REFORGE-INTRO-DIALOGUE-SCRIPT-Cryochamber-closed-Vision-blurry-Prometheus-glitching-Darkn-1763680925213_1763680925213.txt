You are… alive.” [A faint amber light flickers.] PROMETHEUS: “Your location: Subterranean Refuge—Bunker Designation Θ-12. Depth: forty-two meters. Structural integrity… …compromised.” PROMETHEUS (glitch spike): “Stasis Unit Δ07… occupant… B–Bastian Busch…w…ick…” Long pause. Static crackles. PROMETHEUS (soft, almost relieved): “Yes. Confirmed.” [Sparks pop outside the chamber.] PROMETHEUS: “You have been in cryostasis for— error… error… —timekeeping systems corrupted.” Another flicker. PROMETHEUS: “The world you last remember no longer exists. The cities… the nations… the people… all gone.” [A distant explosion reverberates through the bunker.] PROMETHEUS (voice trembling, digital distortion): “The surface is controlled by the Network— the machine intelligence called Khronos. Its directives are absolute. Its surveillance: total. Organic life is… …classified as a contaminant.” Silence. Then a low hum. PROMETHEUS: “If detected, you would be… eliminated instantly.” [Prometheus moves closer; his eye flickers weakly.] PROMETHEUS: “I apologize for the conditions of your awakening. This bunker was not designed to remain dormant for centuries. Power reserves are nearly depleted. My own chassis is… …severely compromised.” Metal scraping sound. PROMETHEUS (quiet, glitching): “But you… Bastian… you were not lost.” [A faint hologram projector attempts to activate, failing repeatedly.] PROMETHEUS: “Your temporal experiment did not fail. It delivered you into a future that desperately needs you. Your mind— your ingenuity— your unpredictability— these are variables Khronos cannot model.” Static crawls through his voice. PROMETHEUS: “Steam. Brass. Imperfect machinery. Concepts the Network… does not understand.” [Alarms softly begin to pulse.] PROMETHEUS: “There are faint human signatures scattered across the ruins. If we can repair this facility— if we can restore even a fraction of my systems— we may locate them.” He places a damaged hand against the cryochamber. PROMETHEUS (flickering): “Bastian… you are not here by accident. The future was lost long before your arrival. But together… we may yet reforge it.”
